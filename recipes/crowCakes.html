Ingredients

  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 (1.5 ounce) package stackable potato chips (such as Pringles®), crushed
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 ¼ cups shredded cooked chicken
  • 4 green onions, minced

Steps

  1. Mix bread crumbs, crushed potato chips, pepper, dill, salt, and garlic together in a large bowl. Mix in chicken, ranch dressing, tartar sauce, 1/4 cup hot sauce, and green onions. Form mixture into 2-inch or 3-inch balls; press down into patties.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Place patties in a single layer in the hot oil; cook until dark golden brown, about 3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels. Repeat with remaining patties.
